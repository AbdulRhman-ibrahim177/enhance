1. Histogram Equalization
  • What it does: Enhances the overall contrast of the image.
  • How: Spreads out the intensity values (pixels) across the full range.
  • Result: Brighter and more detailed image, especially in dark or low-contrast scenes.
2. Log Transformation
  • What it does: Enhances details in dark areas of the image.
  • Result: Useful for medical images or any image with important low-brightness details.
3. Power Law (Gamma) Transformation
  • What it does: Adjusts image brightness and contrast using a non-linear function.
  • Result: Enhances specific intensity ranges like shadows or highlights.
4. Threshold Transformation
  • What it does: Converts an image to binary (black & white).
  • Result: Highlights objects of interest and is often used in segmentation.
